Accessibility

MEGASPACE is designed to make sure it can be used by as many people as possible and meet WCAG2.1 accessibility standard. The web is designed to work for all people, whatever their hardware (mobile phones, smart TVs, and other devices with small screens) browser, language, or location. This includes those with impaired vision, motor difficulties .Web pages are designed for accessibility from the very beginning of project, so I don‚Äôt need to go back and re-do work. Changing settings in a browser, helped me to evaluate below aspects of accessibility.

Many older users with limited fine motor control cannot use a mouse. My website does not rely on the mouse alone; it also makes all functionality available from a keyboard. Website supports dark/light mode themes. Light mode is for people with normal vision which improves efficiency and productivity and dark mode for people with limited visual capabilities and if working for late hours. Users with colour deficiencies can use contrasting colours on web content so they can easily see it.

My website allows to change the font settings so that it does not slow down the reading speed, vision loss, and reading disorders. Choosing the right font improves the legibility and readability of my site for wider audience online.

I consider this to be a success factor and good trade-off between speed and modularity.
